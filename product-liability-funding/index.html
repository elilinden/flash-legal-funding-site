How Product Liability Pre-Settlement Funding Works

Product liability lawsuits — cases against manufacturers, distributors, or retailers of defective products — are among the most complex and drawn-out types of personal injury litigation. They often involve corporate defendants with deep legal resources, making the fight for fair compensation a lengthy process.

Pre-settlement funding from Flash Legal Funding provides a cash advance against your expected product liability settlement so you can cover medical treatment, lost income, and daily expenses while your attorney builds your case against the product manufacturer.

Product Liability Funding FAQ

Can I get pre-settlement funding for a product liability case?

Yes. If you were injured by a defective or dangerous product and have a pending product liability lawsuit with legal representation, you can apply for non-recourse pre-settlement funding to cover expenses while your case is in progress.

What types of product liability cases qualify for funding?

We fund cases involving defective medical devices, dangerous pharmaceuticals, faulty auto parts, defective consumer products, contaminated food, hazardous children's products, and defective industrial equipment. If a product caused you harm due to a design defect, manufacturing defect, or failure to warn, your case may qualify.

Why do product liability cases take so long to settle?

Product liability cases often involve large corporations with well-funded legal teams. These cases require extensive expert analysis, product testing, regulatory review, and often proceed through multi-district litigation (MDL). This complexity can extend timelines to several years.

What happens if I lose my product liability case after receiving funding?

Because our funding is non-recourse, you owe nothing if your case is unsuccessful. If your product liability lawsuit does not result in a settlement or verdict, you keep the money and have no obligation to repay.
