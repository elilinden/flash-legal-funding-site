Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Flash Legal Funding (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use this website.

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Information we collect

We may collect information that you submit to us, including:

  • Contact details (such as name, phone number, and email address)
  • Information you provide in messages or application forms
  • Basic technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and device information)

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • Review and process funding requests and applications
  • Coordinate with your attorney or law firm (when applicable and authorized)
  • Maintain, operate, and improve the website
  • Detect, prevent, and address security issues and abuse

Sharing of information

We may share information in limited circumstances, including:

  • With your attorney/law firm: to evaluate and administer an application (as needed and with appropriate authorization)
  • Service providers: vendors that help us operate the site (for example, hosting, form processing, or email delivery)
  • Legal requirements: when we believe disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud
  • Business transfers: if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction

Forms and submissions

If you submit information through an online form, your submission may be processed by a third-party form provider (depending on the form tool we use). We use those submissions only for responding to you and evaluating funding inquiries.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to operate the site and understand usage patterns. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to respond to inquiries, evaluate applications, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please do not submit highly sensitive information unless necessary for your inquiry.

Children’s privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have provided, subject to applicable law and legitimate business needs.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@flashlegalfunding.com or call (859) 81-FLASH.
