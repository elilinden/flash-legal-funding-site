Our Story

This funding helps injured clients pay their bills before their case is settled, while giving lawyers the time they need to properly settle the case. WHAT IS IT?

What is pre-settlement funding?

Pre-settlement funding refers to the practice of providing financial support to individuals who have filed a lawsuit and are waiting for a settlement or judgment to be reached. The funding company pays the claimant a portion of the expected settlement amount in advance, in exchange for a portion of the eventual settlement or judgment. This type of funding is often used by plaintiffs who need money to cover living expenses, medical bills, or other costs while they wait for their cases to be resolved.
