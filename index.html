WHAT IS IT?

What Is Non-Recourse Pre-Settlement Funding?

Pre-settlement funding — also known as a lawsuit cash advance or litigation financing — provides financial support to personal injury plaintiffs who are waiting for their case to settle or reach a judgment. A funding company advances a portion of the expected settlement amount so you can cover living expenses, medical bills, rent, and other costs while your case is resolved.

Because this funding is non-recourse, repayment comes only from your settlement proceeds. If your personal injury case does not result in a recovery, you owe nothing. This makes pre-settlement funding fundamentally different from a traditional loan — there is no personal liability, no credit check, and no monthly payments.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Pre-Settlement Funding FAQ

What is non-recourse pre-settlement funding?

Non-recourse pre-settlement funding is a cash advance provided to personal injury plaintiffs while their lawsuit is pending. Unlike a loan, it is repaid only from your settlement proceeds. If your case is unsuccessful, you owe nothing — the funding company absorbs the loss. There are no credit checks, no monthly payments, and no personal liability.

Do I need good credit to get lawsuit funding?

No. Pre-settlement funding approval is based entirely on the strength and projected value of your personal injury case — not your credit score, employment status, or income. We never run a credit check as part of our application process.

How much pre-settlement funding can I get?

Most plaintiffs receive between 10-20% of the estimated settlement value of their case. The exact amount depends on the type and severity of your injuries, the clarity of liability, your medical treatment costs, and the available insurance coverage. We work with your attorney to determine an appropriate funding amount.

What happens if I lose my case after receiving funding?

You owe nothing. Because pre-settlement funding is non-recourse, repayment comes only from your settlement proceeds. If your personal injury case does not result in a settlement or judgment, you keep the money and have zero obligation to repay. The risk is entirely on the funding company.

How fast can I get a pre-settlement cash advance?

Many applicants receive funding within 24 hours of approval. After you apply and we review your case details with your attorney, funds can be sent the same day via bank transfer, check, or wire. The entire process from application to funding can take as little as one business day.
