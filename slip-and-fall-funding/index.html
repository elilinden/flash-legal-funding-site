Slip and Fall Funding FAQ

Can I get pre-settlement funding for a slip and fall case?

Yes. Flash Legal Funding provides non-recourse cash advances for slip and fall premises liability cases. If you've been injured due to a property owner's negligence and have an attorney handling your case, you may qualify for funding.

What do I need to prove in a slip and fall case to get funding?

We evaluate your case based on liability (did the property owner know about or create the hazardous condition?), the severity of your injuries and medical treatment, and available insurance coverage. Strong cases with clear negligence and documented injuries are most likely to be approved.

How long do slip and fall lawsuits typically take?

Slip and fall cases can take anywhere from several months to over two years to settle, depending on the complexity of liability, severity of injuries, and whether the case goes to trial. Pre-settlement funding helps bridge this financial gap so you can wait for a fair settlement.
