      <h2>The "Holding Power" Advantage</h2>
      <p>Insurance companies know that time is on their side. Their entire strategy is often built on Delay, Deny, Defend. They know that if they wait long enough, the plaintiff’s financial situation will deteriorate, forcing a cheaper settlement.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Funding neutralizes this tactic.</strong> By securing a cash advance for your client to cover immediate needs (rent, car payments, groceries), you buy yourself the "holding power" necessary to litigate the case properly.</p>

      <h2>Better Treatment = Better Value</h2>
      <p>Financial stress often forces clients to miss medical appointments or skip necessary treatments because they cannot afford the copays or the gas money to get there. Gaps in treatment are the easiest way for defense counsel to devalue a claim.</p>
      
      <p>Funding ensures your client can afford to continue their treatment plan, documenting their injuries fully and increasing the overall value of the case.</p>
