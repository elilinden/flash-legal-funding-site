      <h2>1. Tell Your Attorney You Are Applying</h2>
      <p>This is the #1 reason for delays. Before we can give you money, we must verify the details of your case with your law firm. If your attorney doesn't know you applied, they might ignore our calls or emails, thinking we are spam.</p>
      <p><strong>Action Step:</strong> Before you fill out our form, call your law firm. Tell them: <em>"I am applying for funding with Flash Legal Funding. Please look out for their email and send them the requested documents."</em></p>

      <h2>2. Have Your Basic Details Ready</h2>
      <p>Incomplete applications slow everything down. To process your request instantly, we need accurate information right from the start.</p>
      <p>Make sure you have:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Your attorney’s full name and phone number.</li>
        <li>The exact date of your accident.</li>
        <li>The type of accident (e.g., Motor Vehicle, Slip and Fall).</li>
        <li>A rough estimate of how much money you need.</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>3. Be Responsive</h2>
      <p>Once we approve your case, we will send you a contract to sign via email (usually DocuSign). The faster you sign it, the faster we can send the money. Keep an eye on your inbox (and your spam folder!) after you apply.</p>

      <h2>Why It Matters</h2>
      <p>We are a direct lender, meaning we don't have to wait for outside approval. The money is ready to go. By preparing your attorney and checking your email, you remove the only roadblocks standing between you and your cash.</p>
